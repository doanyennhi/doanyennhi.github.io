I chose to work with these members for this assignment partly because of our personality test results. As can be seen, every member’s personality result is different from each other, even though some members’ results are quite similar to one another. Furthermore, it can be noticed that none of the elements in the MBTI result are too dominant in our group, so the results are quite balanced. All of these imply that we can balance and “neutralize” each other out, and help each other to improve our weaknesses, which is very important in determining our group’s success, since a group may fail to “function” if some members are too overpowering or dominant. For example, there are two Judging people in our group, which are Linh and Manh, and since Judging people tend to be more scheduled and organized, Linh and Manh can help and encourage the other three Perceiving members, who are rather spontaneous, to be more organized and to plan things in advance.

The second main reason I would like to work with these members is the variety of skills in various areas that our group has, and that each person is strong in areas that some others lack. This is very beneficial for our group’s success because we can work on different parts of the project and easily divide the tasks between the members. For example, since I am strong in Academic Writing, and Manh and I are good at Academic Research, we can take responsibility for the theoretical aspects of the project, such as researching how to implement the product, explaining some theoretical concepts, and editing or proofreading the final report. Since the other three members’ strengths are related to coding and programming, they can be assigned the task of making a demo, draft, or prototype of our desired final product.
