IT TECHNOLOGIES

After choosing the desired webcomic, the site will direct the user to the webpage for that specific category of goods of that specific webtoon series, and the products are displayed along with its price and the authors of the webcomic (clicking on the names of the authors in this page will lead you to the specific creators’ page). A filter function is also available on this page to further filter out items based on characteristics like color or price range. When the user clicks on the item, it will lead them to another page with a more detailed description of the merchandise along with more photos and videos of the merch, reviews and ratings from previous buyers, and an option for them to buy the good right away or to add the item to their cart, and they can always view their cart later on by clicking on the cart icon in the home page. If the user buys right away, the site will redirect the user to a page that will display the item price, shipping, and other related fees, and they can fill in their shipping and payment information, or the site may ask the user to sign in or sign up if they did not login before moving to the step above. After the purchase, a page with a digital receipt and confirmation of payment will appear for users to print or screenshot.

This project is interesting to me because I am very addicted to Webtoon, especially to the series “Purple Hyacinth.” Hence, I have always wanted to support the authors through other means apart from liking the episodes. Furthermore, I really want to be able to buy goods related to the series to keep as memories, such as a plushie of my favorite character. As a result, I have thought of making a website that sells merch designed by the creators of “Purple Hyacinth” and other webtoons. The Webtoon company already has a Webtoon shop to sell some Webtoon-related products, but there are several problems with the current official shop: they have goods for only a few popular webtoons, the goods are limited, and the design is not diverse, which are all evident when you visit the Webtoon shop. Therefore, I want to create an online shopping site where the creators of any series can design and sell goods related to their own webtoons.
